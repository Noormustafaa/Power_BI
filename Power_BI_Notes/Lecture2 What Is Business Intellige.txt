 Tools and Processes: BI tools access and analyze data sets and present analytical findings in reports, summaries, dashboards, graphs, charts, and maps to provide users with detailed intelligence about the state of the business.





 Why is Power BI Important for Business Intelligence?

--Integration and Accessibility: Power BI integrates well with existing applications, allowing for the automatic extraction and interpretation of data. This ease of use extends its accessibility, allowing non-technical users to generate reports and insights.

Real-Time Processing: Power BI dashboards provide a real-time view of the business with the capability to update automatically as [data] refreshes. This means decisions can be [made] promptly based on the latest data availa[ble].
