Power Bi Notes:

What is Power BI?

Defination:Power BI is a Business Intelligence (BI) and Data Visualization tool developed by Microsoft.

Power BI helps us convert raw data into charts, graphs, dashboards, and reports so that people can easily understand the data and make better decisions.

Imagine you have an Excel file containing 100,000 rows of sales data. Reading all those rows is difficult. Power BI can transform that data into
Bar Charts
Pie Charts
Line Graphs
Maps
Interactive Dashboards
Business Reports

so that anyone can understand the information in seconds.


Why Do We Use Power BI?

Suppose a company sells products in different cities.

Raw Data
City	Sales
Karachi	50000
Lahore	70000
Islamabad 30000

Looking at this table gives some information.

But Power BI can create a chart showing:

 Lahore = Highest Sales

 Islamabad = Lowest Sales

This makes analysis much faster.




--Real-Life Example

Imagine you own a café.

Every day you record:

Customer Name
Item Ordered
Quantity
Price
Date


After one year, you may have thousands of records.

Questions like:

Which item sells the most?
Which month had the highest sales?
Which day is busiest?
What is total revenue?

are difficult to answer manually.

Power BI can create a dashboard that shows all these answers instantly.

What is DAX?

Power BI uses a formula language called DAX (Data Analysis Expressions).

Example:

Total Sales:

Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Amount])

Average Sales:

Average Sales = AVERAGE(Sales[Amount])

DAX is similar to Excel formulas but more powerful.
