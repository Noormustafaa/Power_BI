Workflow Of PowerBI

 1. Connect Your Data (Data Source)
Definition:Alag-alag jagahon se raw data ko Power BI ke andar lekar aana (import karna).
* Example: Ek retail store ka data unke Excel sheets, SQL database, aur website ke Google Analytics se Power BI me connect karna.

### 2. Transform Data (Data Cleaning / Power Query)

Definition: Raw data me se kharabiyan khatam karna, missing values bharna, aur use use karne ke kabil banana.
Example:Data me se duplicate customer IDs ko delete karna aur "Null" values ki jagah 0 likhna.

 3. Model Data (Data Modeling / Relationships)

Definition:** Alag-alag tables ko aapas me jodna (relationship banana) aur DAX formulas use karke naye business metrics calculate karna.
Example:** `Customers Table` aur `Sales Table` ko "Customer ID" ke zariye aapas me connect karna taake pata chal sake kis customer ne kitni kharidari ki.

 4. Create Visualization (Design Charts)

Definition:** Cleaned aur modeled data ko dekh kar use graphs, charts, aur maps ki shakal me badalna.
Example:** Pichle 5 saal ki sales ko dikhane ke liye ek "Line Chart" banana aur har city ki sales ke liye "Bar Chart" use karna.

 5. Create Reports (Combine Visuals)
 Definition:** Boht saare charts aur filters ko ek sath ek hi page (ya multiple pages) par set karke ek complete interactive dashboard banana.
* **Example:** Ek "Sales Report Dashboard" banana jisme top par profit dikhe, neeche charts hon, aur side me "Year" select karne ka filter (Slicer) ho.

### 6. Publish Reports to Power BI Service (Cloud Upload)

* **Definition:** Apne computer (Power BI Desktop) par bani hui report ko Microsoft ke secure cloud (Power BI Service) par upload karna taake company ke log use online dekh sakein.
Example:** Apni banayi hui sales report ko cloud par upload karna taake use mobile phone ya web browser par chalaya ja sake.

 7. Distribute Reports (Share with Stakeholders)
Definition:** Cloud par upload ki hui report ka access ya automatic email subscription manager aur stakeholders ko dena taake wo faisle le sakein.
* **Example:** Report ka link company ke CEO aur Managers ke sath share karna taake wo roz subah automatic updated sales dekh sakein.
